MOSS (Market Opportunity Scenario Simulator) is a series of assets in development providing geospatial analysis to help understand market opportunity, risk, and market demographics.

The main assets within MOSS are:

  • MOSS Framework, backend structure built with python
  • MOSS Operational Model, generates monthly datasets for agencies
  • MOSS Map, a simple data visualization of the MOSS Operational Model
  • MOSS Analysis Map, a complex map allowing users to adjust model paremeters and rules
