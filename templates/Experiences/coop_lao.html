My Internship Experience at the Legislative Assembly of Ontario

During my internship, I worked as a Software Developer and Analyst with the Legislative Assembly of Ontario. This role gave me the opportunity to contribute to both internal applications and public-facing web platforms, while collaborating closely with a wide range of teams.

Key Responsibilities

  • Supported internal Power Apps used by staff.
  • Contributed to public-facing Drupal-based websites, focusing on usability, functionality, and accessibility.
  • Handled site analytics, bug fixes, and maintenance.
  • Assisted with workplace engagement through social events like cornhole tournaments and the organization picnic.
