Key Responsibilities

My key responsibilities included improving our Beyond Asset Management (BAM) Power App, which we used to track and manage our data and analytics assets. I worked on features that supported asset ownership, lineage, document storage, and our Asset Value Score (AVS) along with its scoring methods.

Major Projects

1. BAM & BET

2. Asset Value Scores Historization

3. BAM LITE

Overview

BAM LITE is a low-code power app designed for individual contributors and managers who need to quickly:

  • Access and lookup BAM assets and evaluations
  • Evaluate assets
  • Create new assets

Purpose & Design Philosophy

Analysis of Bam usage shows that the majority of usage align with the three core solutions outlined above.

  • The top 8 screens (out of 40) account for 91% of overall usage.
  • By condensing these into 4 streamlined screens, BAM LITE delivers the same functionality with greater efficiency.

The core principle is to keep the app LITE, prioritizing efficient queries, simplified navigation, and reduced clutter.

Improvements

  • Efficient Data Handling: BAM LITE retrieves read-only data via direct SharePoint access, avoiding local storage in the application.

  • Optimized Initialization: Refactored BAM’s On-Start (loading) property, removing 97% of its logic and distributing functionality across individual screens. This significantly reduced BAM’s initial loading time.

  • Refactored score calculation logic: The previous ‘reverse-pyramid’ approach applied filters across all data; the new design joins only data relevant to the target asset, improving efficiency and accuracy
